1. Platform Owner (Super Admin)

This is YOU.

You created the software.

You can see every clinic, hospital, doctor, and patient.

Tables
No.	Table	Purpose
1	Organizations	Every clinic or hospital using your software
2	Branches (Optional)	Multiple branches of an organization
3	Users	Login for everyone
4	Roles	Platform Owner, Admin, Doctor, Patient, Receptionist
5	SystemSettings	Global application settings
6	AuditLogs	System activity
7	UserSessions (LoginHistory)	Login/logout history
2. Clinic / Hospital Admin

This is the owner or administrator of a clinic or hospital.

They manage everything inside their own organization only.

Tables
No.	Table	Purpose
8	Departments (Optional)	Cardiology, ENT, Orthopedics
9	CMSSettings	Website settings
10	Services	Services displayed on the website
11	Gallery	Website gallery
12	FAQs	Frequently asked questions
13	Testimonials	Patient testimonials
14	ContactMessages	Messages from Contact Us page
15	Receptionists (Optional)	Receptionist accounts
16	Notifications	Notifications for users in the organization
3. Doctors

Everything related to doctors.

Tables
No.	Table	Purpose
17	Doctors	Doctor profile
18	Specializations	Master list of specializations
19	DoctorSpecializations	Connects doctors to one or more specializations
20	DoctorAvailability	Weekly schedule
21	DoctorLeave	Leave management
22	DoctorNotes	Consultation notes
23	Prescriptions	Prescription header
24	PrescriptionMedicines	Medicines inside a prescription
25	Reviews	Patient ratings and reviews
4. Patients

Everything related to patients.

Tables
No.	Table	Purpose
26	Patients	Patient profile
27	PatientMedicalHistory	Allergies, diseases, surgeries, chronic conditions
28	PatientVitals	Height, Weight, BP, Sugar, Temperature
29	MedicalDocuments	Reports, MRI, CT Scan, X-rays, PDFs
